Electric-Wonderland

Electric Wonderland by Hypernova Bliss is an electrifying genre-defying journey through a hyper-stylized, digital dreamscape wehre the boundaries between reality and virtuality no longer exist. The album explores the joys, pitfalls, and emotional turbulence of a world dominated by technology, artificial intelligence, and digital love, all wrapped in the energetic, glossy, and often chaotic style of hyperpop.

MEET THE MEMBERS

Luna Vex

Luna Vex, leader and the lead vocalist of the band, creates a striking visual presence that matches her celestial-inspired vocals with her shimmering silver hair and holographic fashion. She explores themes of space, identity, and self-discovery, offering both introspective moments and bold anthems for empowerment.

Nova Lux

Known for her high-voltage energy and electricfying beats, Nova Lux is the sonic powerhouse of the band. A master of synths and bass drops, she's the driving force behind their signature sound-futuristic glitchy, and addictive.

Xer0 Z

The enigmatic producer and experimentalist, Xer0 Z thrives in the chaotic, glitchy realms of hyperpop. Behind the scenes, they sculpt the band's signature sound, blending distorted vocals, avant-garde production, and cyberpunk aesthetics.

Haze Neutron

The mysterious, brooding member of the group, Haze Neutron handles the drums that creates different layers and depths to the band's high-energy vibe. Their moody, futuristic fashion and icy presence create a perfect contrast to the more vibrant personalities of the band.
