ELECTRIC WONDERLAND VINYL

29.99 CAD

The album by Hypernova Bliss, Electric Wonderland is a sonic journey through a neon-lit, dystopian dreamscape. Blending futuristic hyperpop, glitchy electronica, and ethereal soundscapes, the album transports listeners to a world where reality and virtuality blur. Themes of self-discovery, rebellion, and cosmic exploration run throughout the record, with each track offering a glimpse into the band’s vibrant, otherworldly universe. From the explosive energy of “Neon Nightmare” to the haunting serenity of “Galactic Heartbeat,” Electric Wonderland is a bold statement of artistic freedom, with glitchy beats, soaring vocals, and unpredictable production that reflects the band’s cutting-edge approach to sound and style. A perfect blend of chaos and beauty, the album invites listeners to escape into the electric unknown.
